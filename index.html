About Us

TNERF is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization committed to restoring and protecting natural spaces through community-driven action. We engage volunteers in hands-on environmental initiatives, including litter cleanups, habitat restoration, and sustainability education, to preserve the beauty of our ecosystems.
